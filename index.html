The History of Durham House of Pet

Durham House of Pet is a local pet store in Durham, New Hampshire, that provides and sells pet foods, pet toys, and online services. It is considered one of one the big four of pet stores in New Hampshire.It was founded by Bill Gates in 1997. For pets' food, the store's products are originally made in the nature without adding any chemicals.

What can we offer?

We are one of the few pet stores in New Hampshire that offer services and food to cats and puppies from local and non-local breeders. Check out the page to see what is currently available. We also offer birds featuring some great services for some kinds of birds like parrots,cockatiels, conures, lovebirds, parakeets and much more, along with the birds are a full line of cages by Kings Cages. We also provide the toys made by locally and a full range of seeds, pellets and dried fruit. We now carry feeder fish, live crickets, superworms and frozen mice/rats. We also now have tiny animals and reptiles. We hope you enjoy our new offering and as we still have the cutest little puppies and our huge selection of fresh cats.
